The counter terrorist

The counter terrorist's job is to counter the terrorist's attacks and defend certain points. The advantages that the counter terrorist has is that he can hide in a spot waiting for the terrorist to come. The dissadvantage is that there are more than two places that the terrorist can go and therefore the counter terrorist has to defend two plant sites.

Terrorist

The terrorist's job is to choose a plant site to go and plant a bomb at. He will have to kill all counter terrorists or plant the bomb and wait untill it explodes. The advantage that the terrorist has is that he can target one plant site to plant the bomb at. The advantage in this is that the other half of the counter terrorist team is at another plant site so the terrorists will only have to kill 2 or 3 counter terrorists.
